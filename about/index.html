About

Hello! I’m Yaw Asante, and I’m thrilled to welcome you to my little corner of the internet. Here’s a bit about who I am and what I’m passionate about.

Journey in Programming and Economics

My journey started with a unique blend of curiosity and resourcefulness. From fixing mobile phones to delving into the world of computer engineering, my early experiences ignited a passion for problem-solving and a love for the sciences.

However, life had different plans, leading me to a detour into the realm of economics. What initially seemed like a deviation turned into a profound discovery. Economics, I found, is not just about numbers and graphs; it’s a fascinating exploration of human behavior, decision-making, and global issues.

Academic and Professional Background

I pursued a Bachelor of Arts in Economics with a minor in Geography at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST). Graduating with First Class Honors in November 2023, my academic journey covered a diverse range of subjects, from Microeconomics to Econometrics, shaping my passion for development and international economics.

On the technical side, I honed my programming skills, becoming proficient in Python. I’ve organized Python for Data Science Bootcamps and interned in roles that allowed me to apply my skills to real-world scenarios, from Tata in India to KPMG in Ghana.

What Drives Me

My enthusiasm lies in the intersection of programming and economics. I believe in the power of technology to drive positive change and that economic insights can guide impactful decisions. This belief fuels my commitment to contributing meaningfully to both disciplines.

Let’s Connect!

This website is a space where I share my projects, thoughts, and experiences. I invite you to explore my work and connect with me. Whether you share similar interests, want to collaborate, or just want to chat, I’m always open to new connections.

Thank you for joining me on this journey of logic, bytes, and everything in between!

— Yaw Asante
